History

Yeagerist Highschool is a school that teach people to learn what is "humanity", "responsibility" and "consequences". We believe that our world doesn't need smart people but caring people who will lend someone in need a hand. Right now, many people help other to become viral or to become famous not because they really care, that's why our school will provide lesson more focus on human behaviour. We learn that our world education system teach about competition not teamwork, all student compete against each other to get A sometimes they even sacrifice their humanity or talent to achieve that. That system is the reason why many student hate school because it's always about competition, or sacrifice.

Founder

Eren Yeager is the founder of this yeagerist highschool. Eren is the only son of Grisha and Carla Yeager, the adoptive brother of Mikasa Ackermann, the younger paternal half brother of Zeke Yeager. After living for 30 years in hardway, he know how important is education and how the current system is killing the young people creativity. He start to build the foundation of yeagerist and start his own higschool. He hope that this school can become a proof that school should be fun and expand people creativity not killing it.

Vision & Mission

Vision

  • Create better education system for youngster.
  • Help youngster to improve their talent for future

Mission

  • Teach student about humanity, responsibility, and consequences.
  • Teach student to be more creative.
  • Teach student about cooperation not competition.
  • Improve and make student more confidence of their skills.
  • Help student to find their skill, motivation, and career.
